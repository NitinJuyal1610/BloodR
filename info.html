Why Should You Donate Blood?

Several people in the world today die because they do not receive sufficient blood when necessary. By giving blood, every donor helps us meet the challenge of providing life-saving products whenever and wherever they are needed

Benefit of Blood Donation !

Health benefits of donating blood include good health and reduced risk of cancer and hemochromatosis. It helps in reducing the risk of damage to liver and pancreas. Donating blood may help in improving cardiovascular health and reducing obesity.

Process of Donation

Donating blood is a simple thing to do, but it can make a big difference in the lives of others. The donation process from the time you arrive until the time you leave takes about an hour. The donation itself is only about 8-10 minutes on average.
